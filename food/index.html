Tangerang's Local Delicacies!

Laksa

Tangerang's famous laksa is a local favorite. It's a noodle dish in a creamy, spiced coconut broth, usually with shrimp, chicken, or fish. The broth is savory with a hint of spice, making it super tasty. It's often served with fresh basil, boiled egg slices, and crackers. Laksa is especially comforting on cold or rainy days, giving a warm, delicious experience.

Sate Manis Gerendeng

Sate Manis Gerendeng is a type of satay where the meat is grilled and coated with a sweet Indonesian-style sauce, then sprinkled with crispy fried grated coconut (gerendeng), giving it a sweet, savory flavor and a distinctive aroma.

Sayur Besan

Sayur Besan is a traditional Indonesian vegetable dish that usually includes a mix of vegetables such as pumpkin, carrots, long beans, and cassava leaves, cooked in coconut milk with spices like shallots, garlic, candlenuts, and chili. Sometimes, protein like tempeh, tofu, or meat is added. It has a savory and slightly sweet flavor from the coconut milk and is often served as a side dish with rice during traditional ceremonies or celebrations.
