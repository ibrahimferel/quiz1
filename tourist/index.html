Tourist in Tangerang!

Air Telaga Cisoka

Telaga Biru Cisoka is a tourist destination located on Jalan Cigaru, Cisoka, in Tangerang Regency, Banten. This place used to be a sand mining site but has now transformed into a lake with uniquely colored water, making it a popular attraction for visitors.

Aloha Pasir Putih PIK 2

Aloha Pasir Putih PIK 2 is a culinary and recreational tourist destination with a tropical beach concept. This Tangerang attraction features stretches of white sand lined with a variety of food stalls.

Ocean Park

Ocean Park BSD is a water amusement park located in BSD City, South Tangerang. This Tangerang attraction offers a variety of water rides for all ages, including a wave pool, water slides, and splash areas for kids.

Broadway Alam Sutera

Broadway Alam Sutera is packed with fun activities, from food adventures and theater shows to super Instagrammable spots. It's like walking through a lively, glamorous New York street, full of sparkling neon lights.
